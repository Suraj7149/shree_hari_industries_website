About Us

Who We Are

Shree Hari Industries is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-performance industrial packaging materials based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. We specialise in producing a comprehensive range of packaging films and tapes engineered to meet the demanding standards of modern logistics, manufacturing, and export operations across India and internationally.

Founded with a commitment to quality and reliability, our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Kuha, Gujarat, enables us to produce high-volume, precision-engineered packaging solutions. We serve businesses ranging from small enterprises to large multinational corporations requiring consistent bulk supply at competitive prices.

Our product portfolio includes Stretch Films, Machine Grade Films, VCI Films, Silage Films, BOPP Adhesive Tapes, and a range of specialty packaging solutions — all manufactured with stringent quality control protocols and a customer-first philosophy that has earned us the trust of 500+ businesses across multiple industries.

Industrial Packaging Solutions

Reliable packaging materials designed for industrial and commercial applications. Click any product for full specifications.

Stretch Film

Flexible pallet wrapping film with high elongation and excellent load stability for warehousing and logistics.

Machine Grade Stretch Film

High-performance stretch film engineered for automated wrapping machines at industrial speeds.

Manual / Baby Core Stretch Film

Compact hand-applied stretch film on smaller core, ideal for manual bundling and palletising applications.

Colour Stretch Film

Custom coloured stretch films for product identification, security coding, and visual warehouse management.

Printed Stretch Film

Stretch film with custom printing for brand visibility, tamper-evidence, and logistics identification.

VCI Film

Volatile Corrosion Inhibitor film for protecting ferrous and non-ferrous metal components from rust.

Silage Film

Agricultural stretch film for wrapping baled fodder, hay, and silage with UV resistance and oxygen barrier.

BOPP Adhesive Tape

High-strength biaxially oriented polypropylene tape for carton sealing in logistics and warehousing.

Industrial Packaging Applications

Shree Hari Industries – Industrial Packaging Solutions Gallery

Welcome to the Shree Hari Industries product gallery, showcasing our comprehensive range of industrial packaging solutions manufactured at our state-of-the-art facility in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Our gallery features images of our complete product portfolio including Stretch Films, Machine Grade Stretch Films, VCI Films, Silage Films, Colour Stretch Films, Printed Stretch Films, and BOPP Adhesive Tapes — all engineered to the highest quality standards for demanding industrial and commercial applications.

Our Stretch Films and Machine Grade Stretch Films are widely used across logistics, manufacturing, and warehousing sectors for secure pallet unitisation and load stabilisation during transport. VCI Films provide essential corrosion protection for automotive components, engineering parts, and metal goods during storage and export. Silage Films protect agricultural produce during storage, maintaining freshness and nutritional value throughout extended preservation periods.

As a direct manufacturer based in Ahmedabad, Shree Hari Industries supplies packaging materials to businesses across India including Gujarat, Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Delhi, and all major industrial hubs. We also export to international markets including the USA, Canada, Sri Lanka, and Southeast Asia. Contact us for bulk enquiries, custom specifications, and competitive pricing for all our industrial packaging products.
