Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Quality

Every roll of film and every reel of tape we produce is backed by a clear purpose and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Mission

To deliver high-performance industrial packaging solutions that empower businesses to protect their products, optimise logistics, and scale operations — reliably and affordably. We are committed to on-time supply, consistent quality, and a partnership-first approach with every client.

  • Consistent bulk supply for growing businesses
  • Affordable direct-from-manufacturer pricing
  • Long-term trusted industry partnerships

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted industrial packaging manufacturer — recognised globally for innovation, sustainability, and superior material engineering. We envision a future where every product shipped across the world is protected by a Shree Hari solution.

  • Pan-India and international market presence
  • Eco-conscious packaging innovation
  • Technology-driven production advancement
